Platform's unique features

Full-frame motion

SimSandal provides inclined movements of the whole player's space - the seat, the pedals and the steering wheel

Traction loss & Drift

SimSandal mimics the skid of the rear of the car, to get the most realistic sense of drifting in the game

Sway & Surge

SimSandal allows the user to configure three different tilt modes, featuring the standart mode, soft and 'storm' modes

Comfortable
